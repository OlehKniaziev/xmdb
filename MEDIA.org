* Video
** Single stream for a single row
** Streams can be polled concurently
** No special syntax (for now)
** As simple as ~select vid from vid_table;~
** Can get the full video by ~select full_func_name(vid) from vid_table;~
** Raw frame data vs container
*** By default: raw uncompressed frames
*** Container: only by using ~full*~ in select
